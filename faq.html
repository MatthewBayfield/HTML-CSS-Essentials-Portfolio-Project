Frequently Asked Questions

If you have any questions not listed below, please email us your question at Tutor.U@gmail.com, and we will reply with an answer promptly.

Tutee FAQ

How do I book a session?

 Just simply fill out our book a session form, after consulting the tutor availability timetable.

What if I cannot attend my tutor session anymore after booking it?

 That's fine, just email us as soon as possible so that we can notify the tutor.

What if I am unhappy with the quality of the tutoring I receive or the conduct of the tutor?

 If for any reason you are unhappy please email us as soon as possible with the date, time and name of the tutor who tutored you. Please explain what it was that displeased you. We will contact the tutor and try to resolve any issues for the future, removing the tutor if necessary.

Tutor FAQ

How do I apply to become a tutor?

 Easy, just fill out our tutor application form, and we will process it and get back to you via email within the next 7 days. You may be required to pass some safeguarding checks.

What do I need to become a tutor?

 All you need to become a tutor is evidence of being a current undergraduate or graduate of a STEM degree, or equivalently evidence of possessing professional experience in a STEM field or in education.

What if I am no longer able to tutor on a given day at a given time?

 That's fine, just email us as soon as possible so that we can notify the tutee. Although if you make a habit of cancelling tutor sessions we may recommend you make yourself unavailable on those days and times where it is problematic.

Can I get a reference after having been a tutor?

 Definitely. We would be delighted to provide you with a reference for your invaluable time spent tutoring. Email us, and we will try to complete it as soon as possible.
