Our Scheme

We offer free, online, on-demand, 1-to-1 tutoring to UK secondary school and sixth form college students in STEM subjects.

Our voluntary tutors offer 1hr sessions free of charge.

Tutors declare when they are available on a weekly basis, and provide tutoring on a first come first serve basis on a topic specified 48hrs in advance by a student.

Prospective tutees

  • Book a 1hr session using the book a session form, at a time on a day when a tutor is available.
  • Specify a topic or problem you want help with.
  • Await an email response within 24hrs confirming if there is a tutor available then.
  • Receive an invitation link via email from a tutor for the session on the day.

Prospective tutors

  • Register to be a tutor easily using the apply to be a tutor form.
  • Anyone with evidenced expertise in a STEM subject can apply.
  • Choose the times on each day each week you would like to tutor.
  • Choose your tutor subjects.
  • You will have at least 48hrs to prepare a session.
  • You will be notified of a session booking and you will then arrange a video call at the agreed time on the day.
