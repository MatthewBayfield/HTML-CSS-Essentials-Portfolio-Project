Our Scheme

We offer free, online, on-demand, 1-to-1 tutoring to UK secondary school and sixth form college students in STEM subjects.

Through the use of voluntary tutors we provide the ability for students to request 1hr tutoring sessions with one of our available tutors at a requested time on a certain day free of charge.

Tutors declare when they are available on a weekly basis, and provide tutoring on a first come first serve basis on a topic within a STEM subject as specified 48hrs in advance by a student.

Prospective tutees

  • Request a 1hr tutoring session using the request form, at least 48hrs in advance, at an available time and day with a tutor.
  • Specify a topic or problem within a STEM subject you want help with.
  • Await an email response within 24hrs confirming if there is a tutor available at that time on that day for the current week.
  • Receive an invitation link via email from a tutor for the tutoring session on the day, as well as relevant information about the tutor and tutoring session.

Prospective tutors

  • Register to be a tutor easily using the tutor application form.
  • Anyone with evidenced expertise in a STEM subject can apply.
  • Choose the times and days each week you would be available to tutor.
  • Choose which STEM subjects you tutor in.
  • You will have at least 48hrs to prepare a one hour session with a student on their chosen topic.
  • You will be notified of a student request for a session and will need to arrange a video call at the agreed time on the day.
